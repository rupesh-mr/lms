Frequently Asked Questions

What is Library Management System?

Library Management System (LMS) is a virtual repository of learning resources which is not just a repository with search/browse facilities but provides a host of services for the learner community. Filtered and federated searching is employed to facilitate focused searching so that learners can find the right resource with least effort and in minimum time. LMS provides user group-specific services such as Examination Preparatory for College students and job aspirants. Services for Researchers and general learners are also provided. LMS is designed to hold content of any language and provides interface support for the most widely used Indian language. It is built to provide support for all academic levels including researchers and life-long learners, all disciplines, all popular forms of access devices and differently-abled learners. It is designed to enable people to learn and prepare from best practices from all over the world and to facilitate researchers to perform inter-linked exploration from multiple sources. It is developed, operated and maintained by The DOMinators.

How to use Library Management System?

In order to use Library Management System, you need to be a student of Indian Institue of Technology, Dharwad. You need to sign-up with your institute email-id and provide the necessary details to get started. If you have already signed-up, login using your institute email-id and the password you created.

I have forgotten my password. What should I do?

No worries. If you have forgotten your password, there is an option called "Forgot Password" in the Login page, where it will send a code to your registered institute email-id and you can reset your password.

I have entered incorrect details while signing up/ My details have changed since I signed-up. What should I do?

If your details have changed or you entered incorrect details while signing up, you can change them by visiting the admin during the working hours of the library and they will be able to modify your details for you.

If I borrow a book, then how long can do I have to return them?

On borrowing a book, you have 14 days to return them. If you do not return within the duration of 14 days, then respective fine will be charged. You will be needed to pay the fine in person to the admin and then only, will you be allowed to continue using the library services.

Can I renew a book?

Of course, you can renew a book, but the provision to renew will be active only two days prior to the last day for return. However, this provision is tend to change based on the availabilty of the books. For instance, if you have borrowed a book and someone else has already registered for recieving the book after you return it, then you will not be allowed to renew the book.

I would like to borrow a book, but that book is not in borrowed out.

You have a provision to enter the waiting list. This means that as soon as the book is returned, you will recieve the book based on your position in the waiting list.

Will I be reminded to return the books?

You can see which books you have with yourself in the website itself. However, just for the benefit of the user and preventing the application of unnecessary fines, a mail will be sent to the users institute email-id 2 days prior to the last day for return.

I want to borrow a book, but it is not available in the library.

You can always suggest new books to be added to the library. A suggestion page is also there where you can fill the details and update the admin with your suggestions.
